Food Menu

Most Popular Items

Adowbong Manawk ₱150
Our adobo, marinated to perfection with a blend of vinegar and soy sauce, will make your taste buds dance the tinikling. Just remember, it's so good you might just end up fighting your own Lola for the last piece.
Singingang ₱200
Dive into our sinigang, where the sourness will slap you awake faster than a nosy neighbor's gossip. It's the perfect remedy for those days when life hands you calamansi instead of lemons.
Kare-kare ₱250
Our kare-kare is so rich and creamy, you'll think you've stumbled into a secret society of peanut butter enthusiasts. Get ready to dip, dunk, and devour, because resisting this dish is as futile as trying to avoid traffic on EDSA.
Lechown ₱400
Step right up to the star of the show: our lechon, the golden, crispy-skinned masterpiece that'll have you questioning why you ever bothered with turkey on Christmas. Fair warning: one bite and you'll forget your name, your troubles, and possibly even your vegetarian phase.
Lumpia ₱50
Our lumpia is like a Filipino spring roll wrapped in a hug, filled with secrets passed down through generations. Crispy, savory, and utterly addictive, each bite will have you saying, "Just one more," until you've demolished the whole plate.
Pancit ₱150
Prepare to slurp your way through a bowl of our pancit, the ultimate comfort food that'll make you wonder why noodles ever bothered with plain old boiling water. Whether you prefer bihon or canton, one thing's for sure: this dish is more satisfying than finally finding your lost tsinelas.
Halo-halo ₱80
Dive into our halo-halo, where every scoop is like a treasure hunt through a colorful landscape of sweet surprises. It's the Filipino dessert equivalent of a choose-your-own-adventure book, but with more beans and less existential crisis.
Sisig ₱150
Brace yourself for a symphony of sizzling flavors with our sisig, the ultimate ode to pork perfection. One bite and you'll forget all about your diet, your worries, and quite possibly your dignity as you lick the plate clean like it's your last meal on earth.
